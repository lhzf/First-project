Blood Type Descriptions:

  • A+: People with A+ blood type have the A antigen on the surface of their red blood cells and Rh factor.
  • B+: People with B+ blood type have the B antigen on the surface of their red blood cells and Rh factor.
  • O+: People with O+ blood type have neither the A nor B antigen on the surface of their red blood cells but have Rh factor.
  • O-: People with O- blood type have neither the A nor B antigen on the surface of their red blood cells and no Rh factor.
  • AB+: People with AB+ blood type have both A and B antigens on the surface of their red blood cells and Rh factor.
  • AB-: People with AB- blood type have both A and B antigens on the surface of their red blood cells, but no Rh factor.
